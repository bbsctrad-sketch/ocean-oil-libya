Featured Development
Libya Hits 1.38M bbl/day in May 2026, Highest Output Since 2013, NOC Targets 2M by 2028
The National Oil Corporation confirmed in its May 2026 report that Libyan output has reached 1.38 million barrels per day, the highest sustained production level since 2013. The milestone comes on the back of completed infrastructure upgrades across the Sirte Basin, the partial restart of the Sharara field, and new contributions from the Elephant field expansion. NOC has revised its 2M bbl/day target timeline to 2028, supported by $4.6B in committed upstream capex for 2026.
Production
Sharara Field Partial Restart Adds 180,000 bbl/day, Eni & NOC Begin Phase 2 Expansion
The Sharara oil field has resumed partial operations following 14 months of suspension, contributing approximately 180,000 bbl/day. Eni and NOC have jointly announced Phase 2 expansion works targeting full 300,000 bbl/day capacity by Q4 2026.
NOC / Eni, May 2026
Market
Libyan Es Sider Crude Holds $3.8 Premium Over Brent, European Refiners Compete for Cargoes
Libya's Es Sider light sweet crude maintains a $3.8/bbl premium in June 2026 spot markets as Italian, Spanish and German refiners compete for limited cargoes. Tight sulphur compliance requirements under IMO 2026 rules continue to drive demand for Libya's ultra-low sulphur output.
Platts / S&P Global, June 2026
Partnerships
NOC Issues 2026 H2 Tender, 220 Wells Workover & Maintenance, Sirte and Murzuq Basins
NOC Procurement Division has released an open RFP for well workover, stimulation and maintenance services covering 220 wells across the Sirte and Murzuq basins. Deadline for prequalified submissions is August 2026. Libyan-registered JV partners required for all foreign applicants.
NOC Procurement, June 2026
Regulatory
NOC Digital Procurement Portal Goes Live, Foreign Prequalification Timeline Cut to 45 Days
NOC's newly launched digital prequalification and procurement portal has reduced the average foreign company registration timeline from 120 days to 45 days as of Q2 2026. The platform also enables real-time tender tracking and document submission for all registered service providers.
NOC Digital Office, April 2026
Production
Elephant Field Expansion Phase 2 Complete, Output Rises to 120,000 bbl/day
The Elephant field, operated by Eni and the NOC subsidiary Agip Oil Operations, has completed Phase 2 expansion works. Production has increased from 60,000 to 120,000 bbl/day, with Phase 3 targeting 180,000 bbl/day by mid-2027.
Eni / NOC, May 2026
Market
Libya Upstream Capex Reaches $4.6B in 2026, 22% Rise Year-on-Year, IOCs Lead Investment
Wood Mackenzie's June 2026 report confirms Libyan upstream capital expenditure has reached $4.6 billion for full-year 2026, a 22% increase from $3.8B in 2025. Eni, TotalEnergies and Equinor lead IOC investment, while NOC's own capex accounts for $1.9B directed at infrastructure rehabilitation.
Wood Mackenzie, June 2026
